What is UShred?

UShred is a revolutionary video capture platform that automatically records your best ski and snowboard runs. No cameras to hold, no buttons to press—just pure, uninterrupted shredding.

Hands-Free Capture

Our beacon technology automatically triggers recording as you pass through capture zones. Keep your hands free for what matters—shredding.

Instant Access

Clips are delivered straight to your phone in under a minute. Review your runs on the lift or share them before you even leave the mountain.

Share Your Shred

Show off your best moments. Download HD clips and share directly to Instagram, TikTok, or anywhere you want to flex your skills.

How It Works

Getting started with UShred is as easy as 1-2-3

1

Get Your Beacon

Pick up a UShred beacon at a participating resort or order one online. It's small enough to clip to your jacket.

2

Hit the Slopes

Ski or ride through any of our capture zones. Your beacon signals the cameras to start recording automatically.

3

Watch Your Clips

Open the UShred app and find your clips waiting. Watch, download, and share your best runs.
